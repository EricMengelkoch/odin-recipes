Southern Coleslaw

Description

It's gonna taste like KFC coleslaw, if you're into that. Sweet and Tangy.

Ingredients

  • Veggies. This one uses carros, onions, and cabbage
  • Mayo, milk, and buttermilk
  • Sugar
  • 2 tablespoons Lemon Juice
  • 2 tablespoons Vinegar
  • Seasonings of your choice

Steps

  1. Mix the beggies in one bowl and make the dressing in another bowl.
  2. Pour the dressing all over the veggies and mix well.
  3. Cover and refigerate for at least two hours.
  4. Enjoy!
