Asparagus

Description

Asparagus is a classic treat for people who've never tasted anything else or people who want to eat healthy. This is sure to satisfy everyone in the family though, so don't worry about Timmy not liking his food!

Ingredients

  • 1lbs Asparagus
  • Olive Oil
  • Seasonings: Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Start the grill
  2. Prepare your Ingredients
  3. Throw the asparagus on the grill
  4. Toss the asparagus with olive oil and seasoning
  5. Grill until lightly charred
  6. Enjoy!
