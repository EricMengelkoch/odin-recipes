Burger

Description

This burger recipe, some think, is the best ever made. It combines everything you'd want in a burger into one delicious, savory beast.

Ingredients

  • Lean ground beef
  • Onion, finely chopped
  • Colby Jack or Cheddar cheese
  • Egg
  • Dry onion soup mix
  • Garlic clove, minced
  • Garlic Powder
  • Soy Sauce
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Dried parsley
  • Dried basel
  • Oregano
  • Crushed dried rosemary
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the grill on high and lightly oil the grate.
    2. \
  2. Combine beef, onion, cheese, egg, onion soup mix, minced garlic, garlic powder, sauces, parsley, basil, oregano, rosemary, salt, and pepper in a large bowl. Separate into four patties.
  3. Cook patties on grill until they're no longer pink on the inside, about 4-5 minutes per side.
